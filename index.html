About the Armory Assistant

This app helps you plan out your 2025 LegionsCon Armory Expierience with different Upper Body, Lower Body, Armor, and Helmet pieces.

  • Select a Category: Use the icons on the left sidebar to choose a category of armor (Upper Body, Lower Body, Armor, and Helmet). The icon for the currently selected category will turn a bright yellow.
  • Choose Your Gear: The options pane on the right will show all the available items for that category. Click on any item to equip it to your character in the center display.
  • Tail: This will turn the tail that comes with your Upper Body on and off.
  • Randomize: Feeling lucky? Click the "Randomize" icon (the die) to have the app select gear. Whether the tail will appear is random, and there is a chance for no helmet to be selected.
  • Clear All: To remove all currently equipped items, click the "Clear" icon (the 'X').
  • Exclusive: Choices with parts that are completely or partially exclusive to the LegionsCon Armory Experience are marked with 'EX' so you can be informed. Those parts (highlighted on the thumbnail) will not be available when the pre-built versions of the figures are sold through Store Horsemen.
